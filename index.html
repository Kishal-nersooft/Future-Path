Two ways to make a difference

Choose the circle that matches how you serve students.

Agent Circle Member

For individuals or organizations working closely with high school students.

Agents can be individuals or organizations that work closely with high school students - whether through tutoring, sports, language learning, arts, or youth development.

As an Agent, you introduce Future Path to students and families, and support them as they begin their journey. You don't need to manage transactions or paperwork - we handle everything through our automated affiliate system.

Each Agent gets a personal referral link and QR code to share online or offline. Whenever a student signs up using your code, you automatically earn commission - with payouts triggered as soon as the license is paid.

This makes the process simple, scalable, and seamless - whether you're working with one student at a time or promoting to hundreds through your programs.

You'll also receive ready-to-use marketing materials, onboarding checklists, email templates, and sales scripts - plus access to your own dedicated Future Path Account Manager to support your growth and answer questions along the way.

Ideal for: Tutoring centers & academic coaches English language schools Sports academies & clubs Music, arts & performance programs College counselors & youth mentors

Whether you're a passionate individual or a student-focused business, this is a powerful way to open doors - and earn - by doing what you already do best.

Partner Circle Member

For people and organizations who connect directly with schools and institutions.

Partners are individuals or organizations who work directly with schools, academies, or student-serving institutions - helping entire classrooms or campuses gain access to Future Path.

As a Member of the Future Path Partner Circle, you purchase license packages in bulk and provide them to schools, networks, or education-focused organizations in your region. This school-centered approach makes it easy to scale access, build trust, and create long-term impact across entire student communities.

You define your sales price for each school and benefit from a tiered discount model - the more licenses you sell, the higher your rebate. Discounts increase automatically based on volume, and previous purchases are retroactively adjusted so you always get the maximum value.

Partners come from all kinds of backgrounds - what matters most is your ability to connect with schools and educational institutions. Whether you're a consultant, an entrepreneur, or an organization already serving the education space, you bring unique value by helping schools empower their students for global success.

You'll be supported every step of the way with a dedicated Partner Account Manager, co-branded marketing assets, pitch decks, onboarding kits, and optional school training sessions.

Whether you're supporting one institution or transforming education across a country, being a Partner means unlocking opportunity at scale - and becoming a trusted ally in your region's educational ecosystem.
